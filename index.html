Indigenous Crypto Currency
Investigation Tool

Indigenous crypto currency refers to a digital currency that is created and used by Indigenous
communities around the world. These currencies are often based on blockchain technology,
which allows for secure and decentralized transactions without the need for intermediaries like
banks.

Crypto currencies?

Crypto currency, also known as digital currency, is a type of currency that exists only in digital form and uses cryptography to secure and verify transactions. It operates independently of a central bank or government and is based on decentralized technology called blockchain.
Crypto currencies use a peer-to-peer network to facilitate transactions between users directly without the need for intermediaries like banks. This means that transactions can be processed more quickly and at lower fees compared to traditional banking systems.

TOOL

One of the key features of crypto currencies is their transparency and immutability. Transactions are recorded on a public ledger called the blockchain.
